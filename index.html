How to Use
Quick guide for logging attendance
Time In
  1. Select your name, choose your Status, then click the Time In field — time is auto-captured.
  2. Click Submit Attendance.
Time Out
  1. Select your name again at end of shift — form switches to Time Out mode automatically.
  2. Enter your Time Out, then click Submit Attendance.
Emergency Time In & Emergency Out
  1. Click the red icon beside Time Out to open the Emergency modal.
  2. Choose a mode:
    • Emergency Time In — use this if you arrived on time but the office or system was not yet open. Enter your actual arrival time and a required reason, then confirm. Your Time In will be set automatically in the form.
    • Emergency Out — use this to log an early departure. Enter Time Out and an optional reason. Status becomes Undertime. Use Return to resume your shift, then submit final Time Out.
Change Today's Schedule (Optional)
  1. Select your name — a Today's Schedule card appears.
  2. Click the pencil icon on the card to set a one-day or broken schedule.
  3. Click Save. Use Clear for this date to remove it.
Good to Know
  • Arriving 6+ mins late → status auto-changes to Late.
  • Working 10+ hours → status auto-upgrades to Overtime.
  • Flex — no late penalty on arrival. However, if total hours worked is less than 9 hours, status is automatically set to Late.  Float — logging not required.
  • For night shifts (12:00 AM), always use the shift's date, not the night before.
  • Click the globe icon beside Date for timezone logging.
Admin Manual
Guide for managing employees & attendance
Adding an Employee
  1. Click Add Employee button at the top of the monitor.
  2. Enter the employee name, set Schedule Start and End Time.
  3. Check the working days (Mon–Sun).
  4. Optionally add Special Day Schedules or a Schedule Note (Flex, With 1Hr OT, Float, etc.).
  5. Click Save Employee.
Weekly Report
  1. Click the Weekly Report button at the top of the monitor.
  2. Filter by Month for a full summary, or by Date for a specific day.
  3. Click the eye icon on any row to view detailed daily attendance.
Employee Actions
  • Edit — Update name, schedule, or working days.
  • View Schedule — See full schedule details.
  • Delete — Permanently remove employee and all their records.
Reset Monthly Stats
  • Click the reset button beside Add Employee.
  • This clears all attendance records on this device only.
  • Google Sheets data is NOT affected.
