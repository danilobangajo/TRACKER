How to Use
Quick guide for logging attendance
Time In
  1. Select your name, choose your Status, then click the Time In field — time is auto-captured.
  2. Click Submit Attendance.
Time Out
  1. Select your name again at end of shift — form switches to Time Out mode automatically.
  2. Enter your Time Out, then click Submit Attendance.
Emergency Out & Return
  1. In Time Out mode, click the red icon beside Time Out, enter a reason, and confirm.
  2. When you return, select your name and click Return to continue your shift.
  3. At end of shift, submit your final Time Out — both sessions are combined.
Change Today's Schedule (Optional)
  1. Select your name — a Today's Schedule card appears.
  2. Click the pencil icon on the card to set a one-day or broken schedule.
  3. Click Save. Use Clear for this date to remove it.
Good to Know
  • Arriving 11+ mins late → status auto-changes to Late.
  • Working 10+ hours → status auto-upgrades to Overtime.
  • Flex — no late penalty.  Float — logging not required.
  • For night shifts (12:00 AM), always use the shift's date, not the night before.
  • Click the globe icon beside Date for timezone logging.
Admin Manual
Adding an Employee
  1. Click the Add Employee button (top right of the monitor).
  2. Enter the employee name.
  3. Set the Schedule Start and End Time.
  4. Check the working days (Mon–Sun).
  5. Optionally add Special Day Schedules or select a Schedule Note (Flex, With 1Hr OT, etc.).
  6. Click Save Employee.
Weekly Report
  1. Click the Weekly Report button at the top of the monitor.
  2. Filter by a specific Date or enter a Week Number (1–4) to view that week's records.
  3. Click the icon on any employee row to view their detailed daily attendance for that period.
Employee Actions (Table)
  • Edit — Update the employee's name, schedule, or working days.
  • View Schedule — See the employee's full schedule details.
  • Delete — Permanently remove the employee and all their attendance records.
Reset Monthly Stats
  • Click the ↺ (reset) button beside Add Employee.
  • This clears all attendance records on this device only.
  • Google Sheets data is NOT affected.
