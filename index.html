How to Use
How to Log Attendance
  1. Type your name in the Employee Name field or select it from the list.
  2. Check that the Date is correct (auto-filled to today).
  3. Select your Status (e.g. Present, Late, Absent).
  4. Click/focus the Time In field to auto-capture the current time (locked mode).
  5. Click Submit Attendance to save your Time In.
  6. At the end of your shift, select your name again — the form will switch to Time Out mode automatically.
  7. Click Submit Attendance again to save your Time Out.
One-Day Shift / Broken Schedule (Optional)
  1. Select your name first, then click the button beside Employee Name.
  2. Choose Change Shift Schedule (single time range) or Broken Schedule (Part 1 and Part 2).
  3. Set the Date and time values, then click Save.
  4. The override applies to that date only; the next day returns to your regular/special schedule.
  5. Use Clear for this date if you want to remove the override.
Timezone and Date Tips
  • Click the globe button beside Date to open timezone options.
  • When a timezone is selected, the displayed international clock updates live.
  • Use the selected timezone when logging for offsite or international teams.
Emergency / Early Out
  1. Select your name (you must have a Time In record for today).
  2. Click the red button beside Time Out.
  3. Enter your time out and an optional reason.
  4. Click Confirm Emergency Out.
  5. When you return, select your name again and click Return to continue your shift.
Schedule Notes Behavior
  • Flex: you can submit attendance normally, and Time In uses actual logged time.
  • Float: attendance logging is blocked (not required to log attendance).
  • Special Day Schedule: if set by admin, it overrides regular schedule for that weekday.
Night Shift (12:00 AM) Reminder
  • If your schedule starts at 12:00 AM (or early morning) and you time in early at night (e.g. 11:30 PM), the system treats it as the next day shift.
  • When logging, select the schedule date (the day your shift belongs to), not the previous calendar night.
  • Example: for a shift starting 12:00 AM on Friday, use Friday as the attendance date even if you time in Thursday night.
How to Access Admin Panel
  1. Click the Admin button at the top of the page.
  2. Enter the admin password when prompted.
  3. Once verified, the Attendance Monitor panel will appear.
  4. To exit admin view, click the Admin button again.
Admin Manual
Adding an Employee
  1. Click the Add Employee button (top right of the monitor).
  2. Enter the employee name.
  3. Set the Schedule Start and End Time.
  4. Check the working days (Mon–Sun).
  5. Optionally add Special Day Schedules or select a Schedule Note (Flex, With 1Hr OT, etc.).
  6. Click Save Employee.
Weekly Report
  1. Click the Weekly Report button at the top of the monitor.
  2. Filter by a specific Date or enter a Week Number (1–4) to view that week's records.
  3. Click the icon on any employee row to view their detailed daily attendance for that period.
Employee Actions (Table)
  • Edit — Update the employee's name, schedule, or working days.
  • View Schedule — See the employee's full schedule details.
  • Delete — Permanently remove the employee and all their attendance records.
Reset Monthly Stats
  • Click the ↺ (reset) button beside Add Employee.
  • This clears all attendance records on this device only.
  • Google Sheets data is NOT affected.
